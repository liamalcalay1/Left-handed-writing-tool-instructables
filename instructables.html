Project 5 Instructables

Assistive Tech

Project Description:

How to build left-handed writing tool instructables including required materials and building steps.

Our Goal

The goal of this product is to help prevent smudging for left-handed people when writing. To verify how effective our product was, we asked left-handed people, who had struggled with smudging, to test our device and give us their opinions. We recieved positive feedback and most said they felt that smudging was no longer an issue when using our product.

Required Materials

Access to a 3D Printer and PLA, computer to download fusion files, USB for 3D printing, one 8mm 2M screw, one 2M bolt, 7M screw tap device, one 7M screw, allen key wrench small enough for a 2M screw, and a pencil or pen to use with the device. Additionally, pliers or a small enough wrench for a 2M screw bolt may be necessary for this device if using PLA.

Step 1

Print the writing device and the writing instrument holder piece. The writing device will require minimal supports.

Step 2

Use the 7M screw tap device to create a threaded hole on the writing instrument holder piece. This should be done on the medium sized hole that is NOT parallel to any other holes on the holder piece.

Step 3

Using the adequate sized allen key wrench, place the 8mm 2M screw in the writing instrument holder piece through the medium sized hole parallel to the small hole. Put the screw through the small hole and into the small hole in the larger writing device, pushing the screw all the way through the hole on the larger writing device.

Step 4

Screw the bolt on the end of the screw poking out of the larger writing device.

Step 5

Place a writing device of an adequate size through the holder piece.

Step 6

Screw the 7M screw into the previously tapped hole. Screw the 7M screw as far as necessary to keep the device in place and at a desired height.

Step 7

Adjust the angle of the writing instrument until you are satisfied. Use pliers or a wrench to tighten the bolt until the device is stable when writing.

Step 8

Enjoy writing without smudging!
